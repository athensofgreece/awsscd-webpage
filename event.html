AWS Student Community Day 2024

Learn cloud skills, connect with experts, and kickstart your tech career!

April 17, 2024 | PUP Bulwagang Balagtas

About

AWS Student Community Day 2024 was a pivotal event designed to ignite passion and build expertise in Amazon Web Services (AWS) among students and aspiring tech professionals, aiming to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry demands by providing a platform to explore essential AWS services, understand industry trends, and foster a strong community of future cloud leaders. Specifically tailored for students, recent graduates, and tech enthusiasts eager to learn about cloud computing and pursue careers in the AWS ecosystem, the event delivered key benefits including hands-on learning through live demonstrations and technical sessions led by AWS experts, expert insights from industry leaders and AWS ambassadors, networking opportunities with peers, mentors, and potential employers, career guidance for professional growth, and community building to encourage collaboration and knowledge sharing, ultimately serving as a catalyst for students to embark on their cloud journey, equipping them with the skills, connections, and inspiration needed to succeed in the dynamic world of cloud technology.

Keynotes

Mr. Paul Sears
Senior Solutions Architect

Paul Sears, an AWS Senior Solutions Architect, provided a clear roadmap for those seeking to become AWS Solutions Architects, packed with actionable information and real-world examples.

Fireside Chat with Dr. Werner Vogels and Mr. Jerry Bongco

At AWS Student Community Day PH 2024, Dr. Werner Vogels, Amazon's CTO, joined Jerry Bongco for a "Fireside Chat" focused on tech innovation and career advice. Vogels emphasized continuous learning and using technology for good, inspiring students with insights into the future of cloud computing.

From Econ Major to Cloud Dev:
Mr. Raphael Jambalos' Journey

AWS Ambassador Raphael Jambalos shared his unique career path to becoming a cloud developer, offering insights and inspiration for students looking to break into the tech industry.

Grow Education and Skills
with Mr. Raphael Quisimbing

Raphael Quisimbing, a seasoned IT expert, emphasized the importance of community and networking in the AWS ecosystem, showcasing the vast career opportunities available in cloud computing within the Philippines.

AWS Basics: Building with EduLearn
with Mr. Mark Achilles Flores Jr.

Mark Achilles Flores Jr., AWS Cloud Club PUP Captain, delivered a technical session focusing on the fundamentals of AWS. He led a practical demonstration, building an application called 'EduLearn' and illustrating the use of key AWS services like S3, EC2, Lambda, and DynamoDB.

Community Building
with AWS Cloud Clubs Captains

Cloud Club Captains from across the Philippines discussed the vital role of tech communities in fostering cloud learning, sharing insights on their clubs' impact and inspiring future tech leaders.
